There are a total of 9 platforms for each team, which consist of:

Arena Center
Fireball Tower Power Source Fireball Tower
Redstone Sandstone Food & Ladders
Armour & Weapons Skeletons Misc Items
There is also a single island at the very center of all the teams which spawns stone bricks that cannot be broken with the fireballs.

The goal of the game is very simple, protect your bed and destroy the other team's beds to prevent them from re-spawning.

Helpful Hints:

  • There is additional redstone in the hopper at the redstone island.
  • You can move the hopper to any island that you want more resources from.
  • There is a nice spot on the skeleton island for your bed.
  • If you lose your bed, you can steal another team's and put it in the same spot as your bed and it will still let you respawn.
